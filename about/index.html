About PetrolPrice.xyz

Live fuel prices and context — built for anyone who cares what petrol and diesel actually cost, right now.

We are building the real-time data layer for fuel prices across Asia-Pacific and beyond. Stations, brands, and countries all publish pricing in different shapes; we normalise it into something you can browse, compare, and trust.

Whether you are planning a drive, watching a market, or shipping a product on top of this data, the goal is the same: less guesswork, clearer numbers, and a map that tells the story at a glance.

Coverage

Our coverage spans Asia-Pacific and beyond. We deepen markets where we have trustworthy feeds and keep widening the map as new sources come online.
