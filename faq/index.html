Frequently Asked Questions

Data, coverage, accuracy, and how to reach us.

What is PetrolPrice.xyz?

PetrolPrice.xyz is a real-time fuel price intelligence platform. We collect, structure, and analyse petrol and diesel prices across countries to make global fuel pricing more transparent and easier to understand.

Where do you get your data from?

We aggregate data from a mix of public sources, station listings, official announcements, and user-submitted updates. We also validate and clean the data to ensure consistency and accuracy.

How often is the data updated?

It depends on the market. Some countries update daily, others weekly or based on government announcements. We continuously monitor and refresh data as new information becomes available.

Is the data accurate?

We aim for the highest possible accuracy by cross-checking multiple sources and flagging inconsistencies. However, fuel prices can change frequently and may vary slightly by location and station.

Which countries do you cover?

We currently focus on Asia-Pacific markets and are expanding globally. Coverage will continue to grow as we add more datasets and contributors.

Can I contribute data?

Yes. We welcome contributors who can help improve coverage, especially in underreported regions. If you’re interested, reach out to our team at petrolprice.xyz@gmail.com.

Is PetrolPrice.xyz free to use?

Yes, the platform is currently free to access. Some advanced features or datasets may become premium in the future.

How can I contact you?

For partnerships, data access, or general enquiries, email petrolprice.xyz@gmail.com.
