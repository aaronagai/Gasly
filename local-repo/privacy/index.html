This Privacy Policy explains what information PetrolPrice.xyz ("we", "us", or "our") collects when you visit our website, how we use it, and your rights under the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (Malaysia) and the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (Singapore) (together, "PDPA").

Contact us at: hello@petrolprice.xyz

We use the information collected solely for the following purposes:

We may use third-party services that process data on our behalf, including:

7. Your Rights Under the PDPA

Under the Personal Data Protection Acts of Malaysia and Singapore, you have the right to:

  • Access – request a copy of the personal data we hold about you

  • Withdrawal of consent – withdraw consent to the processing of your personal data at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@petrolprice.xyz. We will respond within 30 days. Note that analytics data collected via third-party tools may be subject to those providers' own data deletion processes.
