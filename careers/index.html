Petrol Market Analyst Data & Insights / Remote / Full-time

PetrolPrice.xyz is building the real-time data layer for fuel prices across Asia-Pacific and beyond. We track petrol movements across countries, stations, and fuel types - turning fragmented pricing into structured, usable intelligence.

We are looking for a Petrol Market Analyst to own our data, insights, and market coverage. You are responsible for making our platform accurate, timely, and useful. You don’t just collect data - you interpret it, challenge it, and turn it into something valuable.

This is not a passive research role. You are the person ensuring our data is trusted and our insights are sharp.

You will manage multiple markets simultaneously. When data looks off, you investigate. When gaps exist, you fill them. When patterns emerge, you surface them before anyone asks.

Key Responsibilities

  • Own petrol price data across assigned markets
  • Ensure accuracy, consistency, and timeliness of all data
  • Identify patterns, anomalies, and market trends
  • Turn data into clear insights and usable outputs
  • Work with engineering to improve data systems and automation

Required Qualifications

  • Strong analytical thinking and attention to detail
  • Able to manage multiple markets with high accuracy
  • Comfortable with data tools (spreadsheets, APIs, basic automation)
  • Proactive and clear communicator

Nice To Haves

  • Interest in the oil & gas + tech industry
  • A go-getter mindset; proactive and entrepreneurial
  • Experience in data analysis, market research, or commodities
  • Comfortable using tools like Claude, Cursor, and Google Workspace
  • Experience working with real-time or frequently changing datasets

Compensation

Project-based compensation up to USD 12,000 per year, depending on scope and output.
